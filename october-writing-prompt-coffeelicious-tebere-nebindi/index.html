When I got home that night, I noticed the smiling jack-o-lantern in my front yard was crushed. Darn it. My effort, etched into every line my knife cut, parroted back at me from adoring neighbours, gone. Bugs had already begun their own carving of my masterpiece, dragging whatever chunks they could carry back their tunnels.
